Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

At MysticStars, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best horoscope experience:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Zodiac Sign Selection: When you select your zodiac sign for readings, this choice is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Theme Preferences: Your dark/light theme preference is stored locally in your browser.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service, including page views and reading requests.
  • Technical Information: Basic technical information such as browser type, device type, and IP address for security and performance purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide personalized horoscope readings
  • To improve our website functionality and user experience
  • To ensure the security and stability of our services
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and providing services to you.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage: Most of your preferences are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.
  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Access Controls: We maintain strict access controls to limit who can access any collected data.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and local storage for:

  • Remembering your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Improving website performance and functionality
  • Anonymous analytics to understand how our service is used

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect some website functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website may include links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to or restrict processing of your information
  • Clear your local browser storage at any time

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@mysticstars.app

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date listed above and governs the use of MysticStars website and services.
